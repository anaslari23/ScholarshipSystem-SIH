Welcome

The Welfare Department runs various schemes for the welfare of backward classes and the SC/ST. A step towards achieving speedy, transparent & accountable governance. The system links all welfare departments, treasury, databases of Secondary Schools Certificate (SSC), colleges, and banks to disburse scholarships. Processing of students' applications for scholarships, sanctioning, and passing of bills for disbursement are enabled in the system.
